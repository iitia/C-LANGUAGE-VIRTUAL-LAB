A) P
B) PI
C) R
D) D

A) Circle
B) Rectangle
C) Triangle
D) Square

A) scanf()
B) printf()
C) gets()
D) fopen()

4. Which data type is most suitable for storing the area of a circle?

A) char
B) float
C) void
D) string

5. Which geometric shape has its area calculated using π × r × r?

A) Circle
B) Rectangle
C) Triangle
D) Parallelogram
